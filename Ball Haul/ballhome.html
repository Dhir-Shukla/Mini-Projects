Ball Haul

Ball haul is a game I developed to execute my knowledge on app development. It was initially created for my personal use. However, since the reviews were so high of it, I decided to make my game public. To help my users, I release regular updates, with improvements in the UI. With each update, I add new features with different game modes, to entertain my users.

How To Play

Tilt the device in a certain direction, to move the ball. If the device is rotated right, the ball would move towards the right side of the screen. Once the user's ball colides with another ball, the user's ball becomes larger. The objective of the game is to get the ball to be as big a possible. Though, there is a 30 second timer, to make the game more challenging.
